Hello animal lover!

At PetsFriends Wasi, we are more than just a foundation; we are a family dedicated to saving and caring for furry souls in need of our love and support.

Imagine the joy and gratitude a four-legged companion would feel when finding a home where they can be loved and cared for as they deserve. That joy can be yours!

Our furry friends are eager to meet their special someone, someone like you who is ready to open their heart and home to a new family member. Whether it's a playful kitten or a loving puppy, we have the perfect companion waiting for you.

To submit the adoption form, it's important that you are at least 18 years old. This requirement is essential to ensure responsibility and commitment in caring for our pets.

Adopting at PetsFriends Wasi not only changes the life of a pet but also transforms yours. We guarantee that the love and gratitude you receive from your new pet will be endless.

Are you ready to make a difference in the life of a furry friend in need? Complete our adoption form and join us in this beautiful mission of love and compassion!

Thank you for considering being part of our family. We look forward to meeting you soon and seeing the joy you will bring to the life of one of our furry friends!
